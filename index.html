Buzz Mithra-(Freelancer/Agent)

Looking for a simple way to earn without a full-time job? With Buzz Mithra, you can start today with zero investment, work from anywhere, and grow at your own pace. Perfect for students, homemakers, freelancers, or anyone wanting extra income. You’ll get full training, the right tools, and the support of a trusted brand. The best part? No limits on earnings – just use your network to build your income.

FICO MODEL (Franchise Investment Company Operated)

With the FICO Model (Franchise Investment – Company Operated), you invest and Buzz Group runs the business for you. From operations and staffing to marketing and customer service – we handle it all. Backed by our strong brand and proven success, this model offers low risk, steady profits, and complete peace of mind.
